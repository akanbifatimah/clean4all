HOW TO DESCRIBE SANITATION

Sanitation is the process of keeping places clean and healthy,especially by providing a sewage system a clean water supply.

Benefits of improving sanitation

  • Reducing the severity and impact of malnutrition;
  • Promoting dignity and boosting safety,particularly among women and girls;
  • Reducing the spread of antimicobial resistance;

The basic rules for sanitation in public place are:

  1. There should be sufficient toilet facilities.
  2. The toilet facilities should be arranged in seperate blocks for men and women..
  3. The men's toilet block should have urinals and toilet compartments.
  4. There must be a wash basin with clean water.
